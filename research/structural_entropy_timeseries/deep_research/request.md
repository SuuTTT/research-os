# Deep Research Prompt

Research idea:
Integrate structural entropy into N-BEATS, N-BEATSx, and TimesNet to improve long-horizon forecasting.

Goal:
Identify the current SOTA, benchmarks, official code, datasets, metrics, and
reproduction pitfalls.

Return:
1. Problem statement.
2. SOTA table with paper, year, venue, method, benchmark, metric, result,
   training budget, code URL, and license.
3. Official benchmark protocol.
4. Dataset download/setup instructions.
5. Strong baseline repos and commit recommendations.
6. Known reproduction issues.
7. Fairness checklist for claiming improvement.
8. Minimal local benchmark build plan.
9. Suggested first five probes.
10. BibTeX entries.

Rules:
- Separate reported facts from inference.
- Prefer papers and official repos over blog summaries.
- Flag incompatible benchmark settings.
- Include direct links.
